Our World-Class Facilities

Martial Arts Area

Tiger MMA boasts a large, fully matted martial arts area designed for safe and effective training. Whether you're sparring, drilling techniques, or attending group sessions, this space is tailored for martial arts excellence.

Fully Equipped Gym

Our gym is equipped with strength machines, free weights, cardio equipment, and functional training tools. Perfect for general fitness or combat athlete conditioning.

Sauna

Our traditional dry sauna provides post-training muscle relief and mental relaxation. A favorite among members for recovery and stress relief.

Steam Room

Our steam room helps open your pores, boost circulation, and promote recovery after intense sessions. A great way to unwind after training.
